About Me

Since Day One

A young dreamer with a clear vision for the future

Namaste! I'm Harshita Sharma, an 18-year-old student hailing from the serene village of Charrian in Punjab. Currently, I'm shaping my academic foundation at ITI Women Ropar, where I'm learning skills that bridge my present to my ambitious future.

My ultimate goal is to become a Chartered Accountant — a dream that fuels every early morning study session and every late-night revision. I believe that with dedication, discipline, and the right guidance, no dream is too big, no background too humble.

Coming from a small village has taught me resilience, hard work, and the value of education. I carry my roots with pride as I step toward a professional career in finance and accounting.
